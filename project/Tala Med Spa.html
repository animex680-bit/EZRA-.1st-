03 — The Tala Method

Four phases.
One unmistakable
arrival.

Tala (tah-lah) means light in Tagalog. Our method is a four-phase ritual that brings yours into focus — built from a decade of clinical work, refined into something a great deal more like ceremony.

Phase 01

Reveal

A 60-minute deep diagnostic — VISIA imaging, skin barrier mapping, hormonal markers.

Phase 02

Refine

Targeted in-clinic treatments timed to your skin's biological calendar.

Phase 03

Renew

Personalized at-home protocol — actives, peptides, devices, formulated for you.

Phase 04

Radiate

Quarterly re-imaging. We measure the difference, then we keep going.
