## Recording notes for VO
- HOOK line — flat, almost cold. No exclamation. The text is doing the lifting.
- BUILD 2 three-beat — each line a separate breath. Don't run them.
- PEAK — the dollar amounts get *quieter*, not louder. Authority is in restraint. Let the music carry the impact.
- RESOLUTION — calmer, lower register.
- CTA — clean, direct. No upspeak on "follow."

## Lines that must land hardest
1. HOOK: "EVERY DEAD COMPANY / HAD A DEAD WEBSITE." (engagement gate)
2. PEAK 3: "$250,000 A YEAR." (screenshot bait — gradient text)
3. RESOLUTION: "BUILD WEBSITES. / SAVE COMPANIES." (memorability — parallel)

## What we will NOT say
- "make money" / "quit your 9-5" — too templated, channel doesn't use these
- "in 2026" / specific dates — ages the reel
- "easy" / "simple" — viewer disbelieves; we don't claim it's easy
