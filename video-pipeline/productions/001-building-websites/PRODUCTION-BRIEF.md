## EDIT NOTES

- The hook text "MOST PEOPLE / SCROLL PAST THIS." is intentionally meta — it names what the viewer is doing. High scroll-stop rate.
- "NO DEGREE. NO BOSS. NO OFFICE." lands as three punches — each one should hit like a statement, not a list. Timing is everything here.
- The "$3,000 A MONTH" text is the most financially tangible claim — it's the peak of the drop. This is the statement the viewer screenshots and saves.
- "BUILD WEBSITES. BUILD WEALTH." is the parallel structure that makes it memorable. These two lines ARE the thesis.
- Keep the subject's face visible during the "$3,000" statement if possible — emotional authenticity at the most important moment.
