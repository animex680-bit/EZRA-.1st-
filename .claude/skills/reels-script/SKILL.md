# reels-script — The Words

The script is the spine. Get this wrong and no amount of motion graphics fixes it.

## 0x100x voice — what it sounds like

- **First two seconds name what the viewer is doing.** "Most people scroll past this." / "If you're broke, watch this." / "You'll regret not learning this." The hook is meta — it talks about the act of watching.
- **Sentences are short. No conjunctions.** "While they scroll, you build." not "Meanwhile, while everyone else is scrolling on their phones, you are building things."
- **Three-beat rhythm.** Things come in threes: "No degree. No boss. No office." / "$3,000. One client. One website." / "Build. Ship. Get paid."
- **Concrete numbers > vague claims.** "$3,000 a month" not "a lot of money." "One client" not "your first client." Specificity sells.
- **Closing parallel.** The last line uses parallel structure: "Build websites. Build wealth." / "Skip the cubicle. Skip the queue." The user can hear the symmetry.
- **CTA is one word or three.** "Follow." / "Follow for more." Never paragraphs.

## Structure (32-second master, scales to 15s and 60s)

```
HOOK         (0–2s)   1 line, 4–6 words. Names what viewer is doing.
BUILD 1      (2–4s)   1 idea, 2-line stagger.
BUILD 2      (4–7s)   The setup — premise of the topic.
BUILD 3      (7–10s)  The three-beat punch.
PRE-PEAK     (10–14s) Tension line + the reveal cue.
PEAK / DROP  (14–22s) THE statement. The line they screenshot. The dollar figure or principle.
RESOLUTION   (22–28s) Parallel-structure landing.
CTA          (28–32s) One line + handle.
```

## What you produce

`script.md` in the production folder:

```markdown
# Script — NNN-slug
Topic: <topic>
Angle: <one-line angle>
Voice: 0x100x — direct, ambitious, premium
Duration target: 32s
Word count target: 55–70

## HOOK (0–2s)
MOST PEOPLE
SCROLL PAST THIS.

## BUILD (2–10s)
WHILE THEY SCROLL
YOU BUILD.

EVERY WEBSITE
IS A MONEY MACHINE.

NO DEGREE.
NO BOSS.
NO OFFICE.

## PRE-PEAK (10–14s)
JUST A LAPTOP
AND THE RIGHT SKILLS.

## PEAK / DROP (14–22s)
$3,000 A MONTH
STARTS WITH
ONE CLIENT.

## RESOLUTION (22–28s)
BUILD WEBSITES.
BUILD WEALTH.

## CTA (28–32s)
@<handle>
FOLLOW FOR MORE.

---
## Estimated VO duration
Read at 0x100x cadence (~2.3 words/sec). 60 words → ~26s of speech, leaves 6s for silence / breath / peak hold.

## Lines that must land hardest
1. The hook (engagement gate)
2. The PEAK statement (screenshot bait)
3. The RESOLUTION parallel (memorability)
```

## Sign-off rule

Show the user the script before any VO is recorded. One round of revisions, then locked. Editing the script after VO is recorded means recording again.
